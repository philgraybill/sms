Service Offered

The service provides one-to-one SMS message delivery to a single explicitly opted-in phone number. Messages may be initiated by the user or forwarded from a private Telegram group for convenience.

Opt-In

Users opt in by explicitly requesting that SMS messages be sent to their phone number. Opt-in is not verbal and is granted only after clear disclosure of messaging terms.
