About This Messaging Service

This website documents a personal, non-commercial SMS messaging service operated by an individual.

The service is used to send low-volume, conversational and informational SMS messages to a single, explicitly opted-in phone number. Messages may originate from a private Telegram group and are forwarded for convenience only.

This service is not used for marketing, advertising, bulk messaging, or promotional campaigns.

All messaging complies with applicable carrier and CTIA guidelines, including opt-in, opt-out, and help disclosures.
