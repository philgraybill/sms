Verbal SMS Opt-In Script

This page documents the exact verbal script used when SMS consent is obtained verbally.

Verbatim Script

The following script is read exactly as written to the recipient before any SMS messages are sent:

"You are requesting to receive SMS text messages from me at this phone number. Messages will be conversational or informational in nature and sent at low frequency. Message and data rates may apply. You can reply STOP at any time to stop receiving messages, or HELP for assistance. Do you consent to receive these SMS messages?"

Consent Confirmation

SMS messages are only sent after the recipient clearly responds with an affirmative consent.

Additional Notes

Verbal opt-in is optional and not the primary opt-in method. SMS consent may also be obtained through written or electronic request. Regardless of method, consent is documented prior to sending any SMS messages.
